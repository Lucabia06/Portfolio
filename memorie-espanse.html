Memorie espanse

Editorial | App | Interaction_2022

Ibrid artifact for territorial branding

"Memorie espanse" is a series of photo docu-albums that chronicle disappeared or destined territories and preserve their memory. The photographs are digitally expanded through an interactive documentary usable on the dedicated app.The project in question is dedicated to the SanSiro stadium, a historic iconic structure of the city of Milan now destined for demolition.
