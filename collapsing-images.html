Collapsing images

Frame by frame_2022

Pictorial experimentation

This work belongs to a pictorial experimentation in motion design that arose from the relationship between analog and digital images. The generation of each frame took place on a unique surface, so each frame represented fidically is covered by the previous one leaving only a trace in digital. This process generates two final outputs: the physical pictorial work representing the superposition of all layers and its digital equivalent.
