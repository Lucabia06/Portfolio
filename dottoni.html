Dottoni

Branding_2024

Branding for a pizzeria in Matera

The identity of the Italian pizzeria Dottoni is an ongoing project under development, designed based on a modular compositional system. The modules, including the logo, derive their forms from the historic dwellings in which the business is located. The identity system is built around visual
compositions created by combining the four modules in different configurations.
