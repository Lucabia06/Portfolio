Image and local identity

The FRAME project is a research effort aimed at developing a reading format for the narrative directions of the territory based on the images that describe it. During this project, the case study of the city of Matera was examined. Visual narrative material describing the city's history and that used to promote it was collected and cataloged in order to provide a visual synthesis of the results in the form of a video story and a digital platform.
