Archivio Futuro

Creative coding | Branding_2023

Branding For a digital archive

"Archivio futuro" is a project to collect and archive amateur photographic material and popular images, created in the municipality of Miglionico to preserve the area's folk memory. The concept of layering and overlapping that underlies the idea of an archive was used to recreate a parametric linear pattern on which the visual identity of the project orbits.
